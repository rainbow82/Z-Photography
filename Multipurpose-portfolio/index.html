About me

Greetings one and all, and welcome to my site. For those who don’t know me, my name is Zoran and it’s nice to meet you. If I do seem familiar, it’s probably because you know me better with half of my face being behind a camera.


I have been doing photography for almost ten years. I started in 2006 with taking pictures at a local Goth hangout. Once I was old enough (21), and could get into different bars and clubs, I was able to expand my networking and get a different type of an experience. Since then I have been to many local hangouts, clubs, and concerts, as well as some out of the city and state.


I have been lucky to have encountered some wonderful people, and been lucky enough to have photographed some of my favorite bands.


All of my Photography is originally taken and edited by me. I do all types and styles of photography. Special charges may be applied depending on the job.
